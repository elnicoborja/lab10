Three things I'd do the moment I'm in.

Lab10 already has the hard assets — live sessions with 650+ attendees, a real community, and a growing reputation across LATAM. These three moves plug directly into what exists and compound fast.

Strategy 01 · Content

Content Multiplication System — Zero Budget, Maximum Distribution

The problem isn't content creation — it's distribution. Every live session gets clipped same-day into 3–5 short-form pieces, a LinkedIn carousel, and a thread. A weekly Builder Spotlight series creates community social proof and a referral loop. A bi-weekly challenge with Lab10 branding drives organic reach.

I built this exact machine at PlayGround across 5 LATAM markets — zero paid budget — straight to 16M organic followers. The system exists. I install it here.

Target: 2× social impressions in 90 days · Zero content budget
Strategy 02 · Conversion

Community → Subscriber Funnel + Churn Reduction Engine

Lab10 has free community members, talleres, empresa programs, and lab10.inc. These need lifecycle architecture — not just landing pages. I'd map every touchpoint and identify when a free member is ready to convert, then build automated sequences triggered by engagement signals, not calendar dates.

For churn: when a subscriber goes quiet — misses 2 sessions, stops submitting — they get a personal re-engagement loop with a clip of what they missed. Same logic that drove +123% lead gen lift at TR.

Lifecycle architecture — no paid media required
